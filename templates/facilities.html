Our Restaurant


With excessive artwork and atmospheric lighting,our Restaurant creates a pleasant experience.
We serve a wide range of cuisine choices that will please your taste preferences.
Open daily for breakfast (7:30am to 9:30am), lunch (1pm to 4 pm) and dinner (7pm to 11 pm).
